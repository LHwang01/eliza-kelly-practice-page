Eliza Kelly's Story!

Hello! My name is Eliza Kelly! I was born and grew up in Sheepshed Bay, Brooklyn but moved to Canada when I was 18. As a child, my dream was to be a painter, and I continued to pursue that dream as I grew up! I have been painting since I was 8 years old and professionally since I was 16. My favorite art style was (and continues to be) romanticism! I always had an interest and "romanticized" the characteristics of romantic art, such as nature, landscapes, and passionate emotions. In addition to this, I am also interested in the time period of romantic art, being the early 19th century. During my free time, I enjoy reading, vising cold places, indulging in photography, trying new food, and, of course, painting!


Currently, I am based in Toronto and working freelance artist, both digital and physical mediums. I am currently open for commissions and would love to paint for your, whether a portrait or an experimental piece!
